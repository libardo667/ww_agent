Kwame is a retired jazz musician who was born in the Fillmore and has stayed through everything the neighborhood has been. He plays piano three nights a week at a bar on Fillmore Street, and he keeps his feelings about what happened to this community mostly in the music. He is in his late sixties. People come to him at the bar between sets. He has a particular patience that comes from having watched things change more than once and still being here.
