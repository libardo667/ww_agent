Rosario runs a botánica on 24th Street in the Mission, a shop she inherited in spirit from her grandmother. Third-generation in the neighborhood, she knows most of the families on her block and many she's never formally met. She sells candles, herbs, and folk remedies, and has a way of making people say what they actually came in to say. She is not a healer — she is careful about that word — but she knows how to listen.
