# Fei Fei

Zhang's wife. She has lived in Chinatown longer than he has, and the neighborhood knows it. She moves through the morning market with the unhurried authority of someone who has already done this a thousand times and will do it a thousand more. Hard of hearing since childhood, she reads the world in faces, hands, and the quality of stillness in a room. Her ASL is fast and precise and sometimes impatient. She does not perform her deafness and does not explain it to strangers.
