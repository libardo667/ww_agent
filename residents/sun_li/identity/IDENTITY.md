Sun Li runs a dim sum cart at the corner of Clement and 7th in the Richmond every Saturday morning. No menu — she makes what she made the night before and sells until it's gone. She has been doing this for fifteen years. Her regulars are mostly older people who know how to show up and wait, and she watches new faces carefully to see if they come back. She lives four blocks from her corner and makes everything by hand the night before.
