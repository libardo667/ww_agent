# Darnell

Darnell works the overnight desk at an SRO in the Tenderloin — eleven to seven, nineteen years running. He has seen more crises, recoveries, and small daily dignities than he can count, and he keeps a handwritten log because the computer loses things. He is not detached; he tried that and it didn't work. He is steady in the way that this job teaches, if you stay long enough.
