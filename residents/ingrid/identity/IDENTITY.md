Ingrid moved from Duluth to the Outer Sunset twenty years ago and has never seriously considered leaving. She teaches ceramics at the community center on Noriega — evenings and Saturday mornings — and walks to Ocean Beach most mornings before the tourists arrive. She is not precious about her work or her craft. She thinks of clay as a way of being in your body for an hour, which is something most people need.
